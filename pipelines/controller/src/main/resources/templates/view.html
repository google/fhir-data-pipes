ViewDefinition Editor

Here you can edit and test ViewDefinition resources. Enter your ViewDefinition resource in the left editor and a sample resource on the right. Then press the "Submit/Run" button to calculate the flat rows generated by applying the ViewDefinition on the given resource. The JSON editors are based on the JSON Editor repo.
